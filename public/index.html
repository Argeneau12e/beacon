How to Use With Bitget GetAgent Playbook
DCA Strategy
Score 60-100  |  Regime: Capitulation / Accumulation
Use when Fear & Greed is depressed and RSI is oversold. Configure GetAgent Playbook DCA at BEACON's recommended interval. Let the Entry Score confirm the window before subscribing.
Grid Trading
Score 35-65  |  Regime: Consolidation
Use when market is ranging with low volatility and neutral sentiment. Grid strategies profit from oscillation. RSI between 40-60 and near-zero funding rate signal the ideal Grid Playbook window.
Trend-Following
Score 0-45  |  Regime: Expansion / Euphoria
Use when momentum is strong and greed is elevated. High RSI plus positive open interest means trend is intact. Trend-Following Playbook captures the run without averaging against it.
